A Note from our Founder

The Home Base for American Cycling

At NRN, we live by 'One Less Day': the conviction that today is the best day of your life because it's the only one you're promised. I have been fortunate to ride over 100,000 miles, and the most important thing I've learned isn't about the bike, it's about unwavering gratitude for the day.

We don't just ride to move. We ride to honor the gift of the day, to protect the community we share the road with, and to give breath to the miles for those who no longer can.

Whether you're here to win a race or to win back your health, this network is built to serve you.

It's all love.

Kody Bäbler
Founder, National Ride Network™
Philosophy

Connection is the New Currency

We've all seen it. The "Social" platforms that leave us more isolated than ever. The training apps that turn every ride into a data point rather than an experience. At National Ride Network™, we believe that the soul of cycling isn't found in a leaderboard — it's found in the conversation at the coffee shop, the shared effort up a local climb, and the hand-up — whether it's a spare tube or a push to the summit — given to a rider in need.

Infrastructure

We're a Utility Company

We didn't build another app for you to manage. We built a home base for the community — one that allows you to optimize your time and spend. While our community features roll out region-by-region, our member benefits are borderless. From Day 1, every member gets access to our negotiated rates and HSA-eligible performance stacks, regardless of where they live.

We provide the plumbing for the sport — so you can spend less time doomscrolling and more time back on the ride.

Who We Serve

Serving the Everyday Athlete

Traditional marketing is built for the 1% — the podium finishers and the full-time pros. We're here for the rest of us. We serve the "Everyday Athlete," balancing a 9-to-5 and a family with training blocks. The parent squeezing in a ride between morning swim lessons and afternoon dance recitals. The professional who knows that performance in the office is driven by their health outside of it.

You are the foundation of this sport; we provide the infrastructure that keeps the wheels turning.

The NRN Model

Institutional Access

As a professional in the underwriting and finance world, I saw a glaring "Retail Tax" in this sport. We leverage the collective scale of our network to secure preferred access to the world's best brands and pass that value directly to you. Your membership isn't a cost — it's Institutional Access. Most members realize 2x–3x the value of their membership fee on their very first gear "Value Stack."

Giving Back

One Less Day: The Seed Program

That's why a core pillar of our network is our Seed Program. We partner with and support youth cycling organizations through scholarships to invest in the next generation of riders who otherwise might not have had a seat at the table.
